Strawberry Blueberry Mango Madness

Description

Sometimes you just need to steer into the chaos and now you can do so with little risk in your morning smoothie. Leave your preconceived notions at the door and let your BLUES run free.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Frozen Strawberries
  • 2 Cups of Frozen Blueberries
  • 1 Cup of Frozen Mangoes
  • 1 Cup of Whole Milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients into a blender or food processor.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Serve and enjoy.
